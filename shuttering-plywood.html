Keto Ply Shuttering Plywood

Keto Ply Shuttering Plywood is specially manufactured for construction and concrete formwork applications. Made with high-quality hardwood veneers and bonded with strong phenolic resin, it delivers exceptional strength, durability, and load-bearing capacity. Its smooth film-coated surface ensures easy concrete release and multiple reuses, making it an ideal choice for modern construction projects.

  • High Density Hardwood Core for Maximum Strength
  • Water Resistant Phenolic Film Coating
  • Designed for Multiple Reuses in Construction
  • Ideal for Concrete Formwork & Structural Casting
