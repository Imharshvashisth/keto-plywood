About Keto Ply

Home About Us
Building Trust Since 1998

Crafting Excellence in Every Layer of Wood

At Keto Ply, our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the Indian plywood industry by delivering products that offer unmatched strength, exceptional durability, and flawless aesthetics. Over the decades, we have grown from a modest manufacturing unit into one of the most trusted names in the interior and structural wood panel market.

We understand that plywood is not just a building material; it forms the core of your dream home and professional workspaces. That is why every Keto Ply product undergoes rigorous quality checks, advanced seasoning processes, and treatments to make it 100% termite, borer, and moisture resistant. We don't just sell plywood; we deliver peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be the globally preferred choice for premium wood panels and structural solutions, recognized for our commitment to sustainable practices, relentless innovation, and delivering uncompromised quality that empowers modern living spaces.

Our Mission

To consistently manufacture and supply technologically advanced, eco-friendly plywood and block boards. We strive to exceed customer expectations through continuous improvement, ethical business practices, and building long-lasting relationships with our dealers and consumers.

Why Choose Keto Ply?

Unmatched Quality

Using 100% full core and full panel veneers, ensuring gap-free, strong, and highly durable plywood.

Eco-Friendly

Committed to sustainable sourcing and zero-emission standards for a safer indoor environment.

Advanced Tech

Manufactured using high-pressure technology and superior resins for ultimate structural integrity.

Absolute Protection

Chemically treated to be 100% termite proof, borer proof, and highly moisture resistant.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Quality begins at the source. Keto Ply is manufactured in a highly advanced, fully equipped facility spread across acres of land. We integrate the latest European pressing technology, precise calibration machines, and automated core composing to ensure every board is defect-free.

Our dedicated R&D laboratory constantly tests raw materials and finished products against the highest international standards, ensuring that what reaches you is nothing short of perfection.

  • Multi-Stage Quality Control Checks
  • High-Density Pressing Machinery
  • Advanced Kiln Seasoning Chambers
  • Automated Calibrators for Uniform Thickness

Keto Ply, a symbol of premium quality and trust. We manufacture the strongest and most premium plywood, block boards, and doors for your home and office. Trust us for a solid foundation.
